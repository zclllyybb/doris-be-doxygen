\subsubsection*{template$<$typename Key\+Bits$>$\newline
struct doris\+::\+Radix\+Sort\+Float\+Transform$<$ Key\+Bits $>$}
A transformation that transforms the bit representation of a key into an unsigned integer number, that the order relation over the keys will match the order relation over the obtained unsigned numbers. For floats this conversion does the following\+: if the signed bit is set, it flips all other bits. In this case, Na\+N-\/s are bigger than all normal numbers.
