\doxysubsection{详细描述}
Discriminated union of several types. Made for replacement of {\ttfamily boost\+::variant} is not generalized, but somewhat more efficient, and simpler.

Used to represent a single value of one of several types in memory. Warning! Prefer to use chunks of columns instead of single values. See \mbox{\hyperlink{column_8h}{Column.\+h}}
