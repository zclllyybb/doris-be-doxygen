Binary operations for Decimals need scale args +|- scale one of args (which scale factor is not 1). ScaleR = oneof(Scale1, Scale2);

  • no agrs scale. ScaleR = Scale1 + Scale2; / first arg scale. ScaleR = Scale1 (scale_a = DecimalType<B>::get_scale()).
