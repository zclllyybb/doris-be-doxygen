template<typename R = void>
struct doris::vectorized::StaticVisitor< R >

StaticVisitor (and its descendants) - class with overloaded operator() for all types of fields. You could call visitor for field using function 'apply_visitor'. Also "binary visitor" is supported - its operator() takes two arguments.
